Configure your property and a comp, see how target tenants choose.

Build your property and a comparable, set the mix of target tenant segments for your trade area, and see how each segment splits between the two. Adjust amenities, rent, or the segment mix to see how capture rate responds.

Results are conditional on a tenant choosing to lease. Calibrate the tenant mix against real demographic data before relying on these numbers for a committee decision.
