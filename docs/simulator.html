Simulator

Configure your property and comps, see how target tenants choose.

Set up your property and any number of comparables, define the mix of target tenant segments for your trade area, and see how each segment splits between properties. Adjust amenities, rent, or the segment mix to see how capture rate responds.

Results are conditional on a tenant choosing to lease. Calibrate the tenant mix against real demographic data before relying on these numbers for a committee decision.
