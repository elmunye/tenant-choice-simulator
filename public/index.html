Methodological basis
Grounded in published research on synthetic consumer choice modelling.

The simulator uses synthetic respondents constructed as renter personas, evaluated against property configurations using a conjoint-style choice modelling approach. Utilities are estimated per amenity and used to project capture rate across a defined comp set.

The methodology has documented limitations. Synthetic responses can produce directionally useful but imprecise estimates and weaker demographic differentiation than human surveys. For high-stakes decisions, results should be validated with a real-respondent survey on the target market.
